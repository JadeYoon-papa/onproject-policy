Privacy Policy

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data from third-party users. This application is used solely by the content creator (account owner) to post music content to their own TikTok and other social media profiles.

We use TikTok's Content Posting API exclusively to upload music videos to the creator's own TikTok account. No user data is collected or processed beyond what is necessary for authentication.
